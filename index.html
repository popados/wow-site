About Me

I've been a part of World of Warcraft on and off since the days of Vanilla.

Skipping over the expansion: The Burning Crusade, I returned with more strength for raiding during Wrath of the Lich King. My goals were serious through to the end of Mists of Pandaria. Which is three expansions of solid raiding experience.

After that, I took a long break from Azeroth, only returning for Shadowlands during Castle Nathria.

These days, I'm diving into the Anniversary Servers to relive the classic adventures and challenge myself all over again.

Outside of gaming I also explore new tech trends. Feel free to connect with me on social media or check out my blog for more insights into my WoW journey and other interests!
